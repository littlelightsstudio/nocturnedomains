About the Book

A hidden world runs on Nocturnal Energy, goblin ingenuity, and the dreams of sleeping children.

When eleven-year-old Helena's sleepless nights turn dangerous, she discovers a hidden steampunk city beneath Vienna, where goblin engineers create human dreams. But something has gone wrong. A shadow is breaking through the system, turning dreams into living nightmares.

Helena joins a new sleep therapy group in Vienna, expecting little. Then a desperate goblin appears in her room, introduces himself, and leads her into a world she never imagined existed beneath the streets she grew up on. Something ancient is breaking through the dream system. Helena, it turns out, is the only one who can stop it.

If she fails, the nightmares won't stay in dreams. They'll destroy everything she loves.

The Helena Flinn Chronicles will appeal to readers who enjoyed the mythology-infused adventure of Percy Jackson, the hidden creature sanctuaries of Fablehaven, and the magical society of Nevermoor. Like those series, it combines a relatable young protagonist with deep worldbuilding, but distinguishes itself through its Vienna setting, steampunk dream engineering, and a magic system grounded in science rather than prophecy.
