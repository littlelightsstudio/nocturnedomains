About the Book

The dream world is breaking. And only Helena and her friends can save it.

Nearly a year has passed since Helena and the Nightmare Squad defeated the ancient evil haunting Vienna. Life has never been the same: she now slips between her world and the goblin city beneath it, helping her friends in the dream factories and finally knowing what it feels like to belong.

But when a new dream takes a strange turn, Helena senses a deeper disturbance. Dream essences are losing their power. Nocturnal creatures are mutating. And troubling changes in the nocturnal wilderness all point to something unravelling at the heart of the dream world.

Together with the Nightmare Squad and her goblin allies, Helena must uncover the truth before it is too late. Because this time, the danger is not just nightmares. It is the very fabric of dreams.

The Helena Flinn Chronicles will appeal to readers who enjoyed the mythology-infused adventure of Percy Jackson, the hidden creature sanctuaries of Fablehaven, and the magical society of Nevermoor. Like those series, it combines a relatable young protagonist with deep worldbuilding, but distinguishes itself through its Vienna setting, steampunk dream engineering, and a magic system grounded in science rather than prophecy.
